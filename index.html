Listen to the stories and inspirations behind any of the fabrics and explore their origin.

Fabrics of the world is a Pirate Technics project for the Festival of the World exhibited at the Southbank Centre throughout Summer 2012.
The Baobab tree is made from fabrics around the world, 40% are designed and selected by 31 international Masters Textile students at Chelsea Collage of Art and Design.
